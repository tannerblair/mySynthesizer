Reverb

Reverberation--or Reverb--is created when a sound or signal is reflected causing a large number of reflections to build up and then decay as the sound is absorbed by the surfaces of objects in the space. We can digitally create this effect by convoluting the impulse response of a room with our audio signal.

Envelope Filter

An envelope filter provides dynamics to our instrument to simulate the sound of a real instrument. We are able to independently control the attack, delay, sustain, and release of a note.
